What this extension does:

  Makes the buttons in the nav bar reorder themselves randomly whenever you click on them.

Why:

  Demonstrates how to dynamically grab a XUL element and alter it; how to attatch event handlers to elements to respond to events like mouse clicks; and how to iterate through the children of an element, add elements, and remove elements.
